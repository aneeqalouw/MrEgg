About us

Mr Egg is a plush toy company started by Peter John May, who took inspiration from his love for eggs and chicken farming. Growing up on a farm, Peter enjoyed caring for chickens and collecting their eggs. However him and other farmers were and still are often exploited, overworked, underpaid and treated poorly. Peter then decided that he wanted to give back to his fellow farmers and start a campaign where he would sell his eggs and donate the profit to his community and to organisations that help producers, such as farmers, achieve equitable trading relationships. Though he wanted to keep his chicken's precious eggs, and so he decided to make and sell toy eggs instead. As the business grew, Peter employed workers to make wonderful and good quality plush toys, and so Mr Egg was born.
